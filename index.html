Effective Date: January 30, 2026

1. Introduction

TradeVox ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use the TradeVox mobile application.

2. Data Collection

Local-First Architecture: TradeVox stores all invoices, expenses, client data, and financial records locally on your device using AsyncStorage (encrypted). No data is sent to our servers or third-party cloud services.

Optional AI Features: If you use the AI receipt scanner or voice invoicing features, audio files and images are temporarily sent to OpenAI's API for processing. These files are deleted immediately after processing and are not stored by TradeVox or OpenAI beyond the API call.

3. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • No user accounts or login credentials
  • No analytics or tracking pixels
  • No email addresses or contact information (unless you voluntarily enter them for invoicing purposes)
  • No location tracking
  • No behavioral data or usage patterns

4. Third-Party Services

OpenAI API (Optional): Used only for AI-powered receipt scanning and voice-to-invoice conversion. OpenAI processes data according to their Privacy Policy.

Expo Framework: TradeVox is built with Expo (React Native). Expo does not collect personal data from our app unless you explicitly opt-in to error reporting (disabled by default).

5. Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control:

  • You can export all data as CSV/JSON at any time
  • You can delete the app and all data will be removed from your device
  • No central database exists, so there's nothing to request deletion from

6. Children's Privacy

TradeVox is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page with a new "Effective Date."

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at: privacy@tradevox.uk

Common Questions

Q: How do I create my first invoice?

A: Tap the orange "+" button on the Dashboard, enter client details, add line items (labor/materials), and tap "Save as Draft" or "Mark as Sent".

Q: How does the CIS tax calculator work?

A: TradeVox automatically applies UK Construction Industry Scheme rules. Mark line items as "Labor" (20% deduction) or "Materials" (0% deduction).

Q: Where is my data stored?

A: All data is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage (encrypted). Nothing is sent to cloud servers.

Q: Does TradeVox work offline?

A: Absolutely. TradeVox is built for job sites with poor connectivity. All features work offline except AI receipt scanning.
